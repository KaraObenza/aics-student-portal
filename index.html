Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

The Requirements are Photocopy of PHILIPPINE STATISTICS OFFICE (PSA) Or NSO Birth Certificate and GRADE 10 ID.

How can i apply to the school?

Simple, by pre-register your information which you can find the link in INQUIRES navigator, or by coming personal in the AICS Montalban School so you can see the environment of the school, ask questions that you want to ask personally to the admin.

Is there an Entrance Exam and average grade requirement?

There will be no entrance exam and average grade requirement needed to apply in AICS Montalban.

Is there Monthly or Quarterly fees?

for public completers, there will be no any Monthly or Quarterly fees.

What is schedule the AICS Montalban open so i can walk in?

The schedule of the school is Monday - Friday, 9am - 4pm.

What Strands does the school Offer?

the only available Strand in AICS Montalban is ICT, ABM, GAS, HUMSS.

Is there school uniform policy?

Yes, School uniform is a must to enter in School. Guard will not allow students to enter the school who don't follow the proper dress code.

What are the procedures for reporting bullying or harassment?

We have clear policies and procedures for reporting bullying, harassment, or any concerns related to student safety. Students and parents can report incidents to teachers, counselors, administrators.

What safety measures are in place at the school?

The safety and security of our students and staff are top priorities. We have implemented various safety measures, including surveillance systems, and emergency response protocols.

Are there special events or traditions at the school?

Yes, we have various special events and traditions throughout the school year, including cultural celebrations, sports tournaments, academic competitions, and graduation ceremonies. Stay updated with our school calendar for upcoming events.
